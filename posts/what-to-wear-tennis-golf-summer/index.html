What to Wear: Tennis and Golf in Summer

2026-07-08

Proper athletic wear enhances performance and comfort during tennis and golf. Summer temperatures require special consideration for fabric and fit.

Key Fabric Features

Choose moisture-wicking materials that pull sweat away from your skin. Breathable fabrics like mesh keep you cool. Look for UV protection in summer wear.

Tennis Apparel

Tennis demands freedom of movement. Choose fitted shorts or skirts that allow full range of motion. Lightweight tops should be sleeveless or have short sleeves. Proper court shoes provide ankle support.

Golf Attire

Golf requires a more polished appearance. Many courses have dress codes. Collared shirts are typically required. Choose lightweight fabrics for warm weather comfort.

Accessories Matter

A wide-brimmed hat shields you from the sun. Moisture-wicking socks keep feet dry. Sunglasses protect eyes and reduce glare.

Comfort First

The best outfit is one that makes you feel confident and comfortable. You’ll perform better when you’re not worried about your clothing.
