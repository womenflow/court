There's a special kind of confidence that comes from walking onto a tennis court in an outfit that looks good *and* works hard. And then there's the other kind of day — where you're peeling a soaked shirt off your back by the second set, wondering where it all went wrong.

Summer tennis is a fabric problem disguised as a fashion problem. Here's how to look cute and actually survive the heat.

![Woman in a stylish summer tennis outfit](/images/court-s1.png)

## Rule #1: Fabric Beats Everything
That adorable cotton top? It'll drink your sweat and hold onto it like a grudge. **Reach for moisture-wicking, breathable synthetics** — they pull sweat away and dry fast, so you stay cool and the outfit stays cute. This one choice fixes 80% of hot-court misery.

## The Dress vs. Set Debate
- **The tennis dress:** one-and-done, effortlessly polished, built-in shorts. The "I woke up like this" of court style.
- **The two-piece set:** more flexible, mix-and-match, easier to layer. Great if you like options.

Honestly? Own one of each. Some days you want zero decisions; some days you want control.

## Colors That Work With the Sun
White is classic — and it won't turn into a personal sauna the way black does. But don't be afraid of soft pastels or a punchy accent. **The trick:** avoid heavy dark colors on the hottest days. They absorb heat and show every drop.

![Woman playing tennis on a bright summer day](/images/court-s2.png)

## Don't Forget the Supporting Cast
The outfit is only half of it:
- **A supportive sports bra** — non-negotiable for the movement tennis demands.
- **A visor or cap** — keeps sun out of your eyes and hair off your face.
- **Real tennis shoes** — court-specific soles protect your ankles on quick lateral moves. Running shoes are *not* the same thing.
- **A light layer** for before and after, when the AC hits.

## The One Thing People Skip
Sunscreen. You're outside, moving, for an hour-plus. **Sweat-resistant SPF** is part of the outfit now — reapply between sets. Future-you, with great skin, says thanks.

## Play in Something You Love
Here's the real secret: when you feel good in what you're wearing, you play looser and have more fun. Cute *and* sweat-proof isn't a compromise — it's just smart. **Now go win the outfit, and maybe the match too.**
